Pomegranate tree

There is a breath. Then there is a feeling undescribable.

Petals are falling, like ashes, or like snow,

underneath the pomegranate tree.

It brings me back to everything I have ever felt,

in this place. Everything I have learnt 

and learnt to forget. Passing moments linger.

The wind gently nudges me abroad, forward.

I stare into the abyss, hoping to grasp another gape of air.
